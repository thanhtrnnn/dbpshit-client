Table: Employee

+-------------+----------------+
| Column Name | Type           |
+-------------+----------------+
| EmpID       | int            |
| FullName    | varchar(100)   |
| Dept        | varchar(50)    |
| Salary      | decimal(10,2)  |
| HireDate    | date           |
| Email       | varchar(100)   |
+-------------+----------------+

EmpID is the unique identifier of each employee.

FullName is the employee’s full name.

Dept is the department name where the employee works.

Salary is the employee’s current salary.

HireDate is the date when the employee was hired.

Email is the employee’s email address.

Each row in this table represents one employee and their basic information.


Task:
