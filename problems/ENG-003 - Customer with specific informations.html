Table: Customer

+--------------+--------------+
| Column Name  | Type         |
+--------------+--------------+
| CustomerID   | int          |
| CustomerName | varchar(100) |
| City         | varchar(50)  |
| Country      | varchar(50)  |
| Phone        | varchar(20)  |
+--------------+--------------+

CustomerID is the unique identifier of each customer.

CustomerName is the customer’s full name.

City is the city where the customer lives.

Country is the customer’s country.

Phone is the customer’s phone number.

Each row in this table represents one customer and their basic contact information.


Task:

Write an SQL query to return all columns of customers who live in the city 'London', and have an even CustomerID.
